Privacy Policy

We only collect your phone number when you text a location identifier (e.g., 82230) to report a vending-machine issue.

Use of Data: The number is used solely to log the issue and send a confirmation to the technician. No marketing messages are ever sent.

Opt-Out: Reply STOP (or UNSUBSCRIBE) to any message to be removed from future alerts.

Retention: Phone numbers are kept only as long as the issue record exists in our internal log. We do not sell or share mobile information with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes.

Your data is protected under applicable laws (e.g., TCPA, CCPA). For questions, contact support@vendgo.co.
